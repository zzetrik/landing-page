The Mongolz.

The Mongolz is a professional CS2 team based in Mongolia, known for their aggressive playstyle and strong team synergy. The team has represented Asia in major events.

The Team

Blitz is the team captain, known for his strategic mindset and leadership. His decision-making in high-pressure moments guides Mongolz through tough situations, making him a key figure in their success.

910 brings stability and sharp decision-making to the team. His strong positioning and ability to control defensive situations make him a reliable player who consistently delivers in crucial rounds.

Mzinho is a skilled rifler who has impressive aim. His versatility allows him to adapt to different roles, whether fragging or holding angles, and he consistently delivers solid performances for the team.

Senzu is known for his precise shooting and excellent game sense. He plays a crucial role in executing team strategies, utilizing utility effectively, and controlling key moments in the game.

Techno is an aggressive entry fragger who excels at creating space for the team. His fast playstyle and ability to disrupt opponents early in rounds make him an important part of Mongolzs offense.
